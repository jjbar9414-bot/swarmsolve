🚫 What We Don't Do (Yet)

We believe in transparency. These are problems people ask about that don't fit our model today:

  • ❌Cloud cost management — Cloud waste is mostly about unused resources and provisioning, not code optimization. Use FinOps tools like CloudHealth or Kubecost instead.
  • ❌Regulated data optimization — Healthcare (HIPAA) and banking (PCI DSS) data can't be shared on a platform. We'll build private isolated environments for this — but not yet.
  • ❌SQL query optimization — Database queries depend on your specific schema, indexes, and data distribution. This requires context we can't abstract away.
  • ❌Prediction models — Demand forecasting, delivery ETAs, and similar ML tasks need your historical data. They're training problems, not algorithm optimization problems.
  • ❌"Reduce hallucinations" — LLM accuracy is a research problem with no clear evaluator function. We optimize code, not model weights.
